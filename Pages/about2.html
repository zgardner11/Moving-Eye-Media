FAQ

How many years of experience do you have?
I have over 6 years of experience producing commercial media, including weddings, real estate, and local business campaigns. I have also spent the last 3 years of those years building my own custom drones and flying as an FAA part 107 pilot.
Which areas do you cover?
The main cities I cover are San Clemente, Laguna Beach, Newport Beach, Dana Point and San Diego but I am willing to travel anywhere in Orange County, California.
What are your rates?
My rates vary based on the desired services and size of the property, as well as the industry. Pricing information can be found by clicking the "Let's Fly" button in the navigation menu. Any service seen on my website can be performed a la carte as well, so feel free to contact me if you're interested in something specific and I can give you a quote. Custom orders are welcome!
What kind of drone do you have?
A combination of hand-built and pre-built drones are used, depending on the application. Standard consumer drones are ideal for exterior angles, but are incapable of producing interior shots due to their size, GPS requirement and other software restrictions. So we overcame this limitation by building our own. Each model used indoors is under 250g and fits in the palm of your hand. This small size combined with the skill of our FAA certified pilots heavily mitigates any possible damage, and we have $2 million in liability insurance on top of all that.
